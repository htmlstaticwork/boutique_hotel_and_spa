Getting Here

Your Journey to Serenara

✈️

By Air

Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) is 45km away — approximately 75 minutes. Complimentary private transfer included with every booking.

🚗

By Road

From Ubud town centre: 12 minutes by car. GPS coordinates: 8.506°S, 115.262°E. Our driver can arrange pickup from anywhere in Bali.

🛶

From Nusa Dua

Private boat from Sanur to Padangbai (45 min), then driver to Ubud (30 min). A scenic coastal journey we're happy to arrange.
