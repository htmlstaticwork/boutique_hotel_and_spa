When to Visit

The Rhythms of Bali

Each season in Bali has its own magic. Our experiences are thoughtfully adapted to the island's natural rhythms throughout the year.

Apr–Oct

Dry Season — The Golden Time

Crystal skies, vibrant green terraces. Peak season for treks, temple visits, and outdoor ceremonies. Book 3+ months ahead.

Nov–Mar

Wet Season — The Lush Monsoon

Daily tropical showers turn the island impossibly green. Best spa retreats, cooking classes, and intimate experiences. Fewer crowds.

Mar · Oct

Nyepi & Galungan — Sacred Festivals

Witness the island's most sacred ceremonies. Nyepi (Day of Silence) is an utterly unique experience nowhere else on earth.
